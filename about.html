About the NGO

InAmigos Foundation was founded by Govind Shukla and describes itself as a Section 8 registered nonprofit working for social development across India.

The foundation highlights registrations and certifications including 80G, 12A, CSR-1, NITI Aayog registration, and ISO 9001:2015 certification.

Purpose

Community Support

Providing food, clothing, and social support to vulnerable communities.

Education

Helping underprivileged children through literacy, life skills, and learning support.

Inclusion

Promoting dignity, opportunity, and sustainable change for people and communities.
