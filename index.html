InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 nonprofit organization working across India to support food access, education, animal welfare, women empowerment, environmental action, and skill development.

Introduction

Founded on September 23, 2020, InAmigos Foundation is based in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. The organization focuses on creating positive social change through sustainable and inclusive community initiatives.
