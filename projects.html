Projects That Create Change

InAmigos Foundation works across food support, education, animal welfare, women empowerment, environmental sustainability, and employability to build a more inclusive and compassionate society.

Our Ongoing Projects

Project Seva

Project Seva supports underprivileged communities through food and clothing distribution, focusing on dignity, care, and essential relief for people in need.

InAmigos Foundation says this initiative has distributed over 50,000+ meals and clothing items to vulnerable communities.

Impact: 50,000+ meals and clothing distributed

Project Bachpanshala

Project Bachpanshala works to bridge educational gaps for underprivileged children through school support, digital literacy, and life-skills learning.

The initiative is designed to help children learn with confidence and gain stronger opportunities for the future.

Focus: education, digital literacy, and life skills

Project Jeev

Project Jeev is dedicated to animal welfare through feeding, rescue support, shelter care, and compassion for stray animals.

The foundation says the project feeds 50+ stray animals daily and supports dogs, cats, cows, and other animals in need.

Impact: 50+ stray animals fed daily

Project Udaan

Project Udaan empowers women through skill development, self-help group collaboration, financial awareness, and menstrual hygiene education.

It focuses especially on helping women in rural communities build confidence, independence, and long-term opportunity.

Focus: women’s empowerment and financial independence

Project Prakriti

Project Prakriti promotes sustainability and environmental conservation through plantation drives, eco-friendly awareness, and support for greener practices.

InAmigos Foundation says this initiative has helped plant 20,000+ saplings as part of its environmental efforts.

Impact: 20,000+ saplings planted

Project Vikas

Project Vikas supports employability and skill development through internships and practical training in areas such as digital marketing, finance, research, content writing, social work, and more.

The foundation says it has trained 30,000+ interns over the last four years, and one project page highlights 1,000+ internship opportunities.

Impact: 30,000+ interns trained
